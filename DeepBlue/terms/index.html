Terms of Use

DeepBlue is a psychoeducational tool. It is not a clinical assessment, diagnostic instrument, or medical device. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

DeepBlue is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact your local emergency services or a crisis helpline immediately.

Users must be 16 years of age or older to play.

By playing DeepBlue, you agree to these terms.

Privacy Policy

What data we collect

  • Display name and avatar choices
  • Game responses (answers to psychological profiling questions)
  • Challenge scores and completion data
  • Real name and phone number (collected at the end of the game, with your explicit consent)

How we use your data

  • To generate your personalised psychological profile
  • For research purposes (always anonymised)
  • To improve the game experience

Data storage

Data is stored in Supabase (cloud-hosted PostgreSQL). All data is encrypted in transit via TLS.

Data sharing

Anonymised data may be used in academic publications. Individual data is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

Data retention

Your data is stored until you request deletion. To request deletion of your data, email wilfred.desouza1996@gmail.com.

Research Consent

Anonymised game data may be used for research in computational psychiatry and psychoeducation. Published research will never identify individual participants.

Consent is obtained at the end of the game via an explicit checkbox before your profile is revealed. You may play the game without providing any identifying information.

Clinical Disclaimer

DeepBlue is created by Weave — Centre for Integrative Psychiatry.

The game uses frameworks from computational psychiatry (the Toroidal Model of Psychosis and Affect) and analytical psychology (Jung) for educational purposes only. Profile results are directional, not diagnostic.

If you are in crisis, contact your local emergency services or a crisis helpline. The creators are not liable for any decisions made based on game results.

Cookie & Storage Policy

  • We use localStorage to save game progress on your device.
  • We use Supabase for persistent data storage.
  • We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking.
