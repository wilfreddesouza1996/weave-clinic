How It Works

Explore 7 islands. Each island has characters to talk to, questions to answer honestly, and a challenge to complete.

What To Expect

~15 minutes per island. No right or wrong answers — just honest ones. Your choices build a psychological profile at the end.

Pro Tip

Go with your gut. The first answer that comes to mind is usually the most revealing. Your progress saves automatically.

DeepBlue is built by a psychiatry resident as a psychoeducational research tool. It's a work in progress — if anything gets stuck, just hit reload. Your progress saves automatically up to your last checkpoint.
