How we work

No two people are the same. So no two care plans look the same either. Some people need medication. Some need therapy. Most need both — done well, done together, by the same team.

  • We listen first. Your first session is long enough to actually understand what's happening.
  • We tailor everything. Your plan is built around your life, your goals, and what you're ready for.
  • We do the deeper work. Therapy isn't optional here. It's central to how we practise.
  • We stay accessible. All sessions happen over secure video. No commute. No waiting rooms.

What we offer

Psychiatric Assessment

A thorough evaluation over secure video. We look at your current concerns, your history, and how things developed over time — and give you a clear picture of what's going on.

Who it's for

Anyone seeking clarity. Maybe you've been struggling for a while and want answers. Maybe you have a diagnosis but something doesn't feel right. Maybe you're starting from scratch.

What to expect

First session: 45–60 minutes. Follow-ups: 30–45 minutes. This is the starting point for most people who come to Weave.

Dr. Wilfred (Digital Support) Dr. Niharika

Psychotherapy

Ongoing, structured therapy — not just advice or breathing exercises. We use evidence-based approaches including Schema Therapy, CBT, DBT-informed skills, Motivational Interviewing, and Emotion-Focused Therapy. The approach depends on what fits you best.

Who it's for

People who want to understand their patterns — not just manage symptoms. If you keep ending up in the same cycles with relationships, work, mood, or self-worth, therapy is where that changes.

What to expect

Sessions are regular — usually weekly or fortnightly. We set goals together and track progress honestly.

Dr. Wilfred (Digital Support)

Couples & Relationship Work

A safe space for two people to be honest with each other — with someone trained to guide that conversation. We work on communication, patterns that keep showing up, and what each person actually needs.

Who it's for

Couples going through a difficult stretch. Partners who keep having the same fight. People considering marriage and wanting to build a strong foundation. The online format works well here — both of you can join from your own space if that feels easier.

What to expect

Sessions are longer to give both people room to speak. We focus on patterns, not blame.

Dr. Niharika

Child & Adolescent Mental Health

Assessment and ongoing support for children and young people — with parents involved every step of the way.

Who it's for

If your child is struggling at school, withdrawing socially, having emotional outbursts, or just seems different from how they used to be — it's worth exploring. Reaching out is not overreacting. It's paying attention.

What to expect

We start with a detailed assessment that includes time with your child and time with you. Parent guidance is part of every plan. You won't be left guessing what to do at home.

Dr. Niharika

School & College Programs

Weave comes to your institution. We run mental health awareness workshops, train teachers to recognise early signs of distress, and design student wellness programs.

Who it's for

Schools and colleges that take student wellbeing seriously — not as a checkbox, but as a culture.

What to expect

Programs are tailored to your institution's needs. We work with your staff, not around them. This is community investment — something we care about beyond the clinic.

Dr. Wilfred (Digital Support) & Dr. Niharika

Corporate Wellness

Workshops for organisations — on mental health awareness, stress management, burnout prevention, and building psychologically safer workplaces.

Who it's for

Companies and teams that want to go beyond a token wellness day.

What to expect

Every workshop is designed around your organisation's specific needs and challenges. We don't do cookie-cutter presentations.

Dr. Wilfred (Digital Support) & Dr. Niharika

Group Sessions

Free online group sessions, held twice a month. Topics rotate — stress, relationships, understanding how your mind works, and more. Community is its own kind of medicine.

Who it's for

Anyone. You don't need to be a Weave client. You don't need a diagnosis. You just need to be curious, or going through something.

What to expect

A small, respectful group. A topic. An honest conversation. No pressure to share if you'd rather listen.
