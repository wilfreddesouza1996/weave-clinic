What makes Weave different

We don't guess.

Neurobiology, neuropsychology, and the right kind of therapy for your brain — not a one-size-fits-all approach.

Medication without pressure.

If medication comes up, we'll explain everything — what it does, what to expect, what the alternatives are. You decide. Always.

Deliberately small.

We keep our practice intentionally small so every person gets the time and attention they deserve.

Real connection, even online.

Our appointments happen over secure video, but the depth stays the same. You'll feel heard — not like you're talking to a screen.

Community as care.

Free online group meetings, school programs, and workshops — because growth doesn't only happen one-on-one.

More than just getting better.

We help you build a life that feels like yours — using therapy, science, and honest conversation together.
