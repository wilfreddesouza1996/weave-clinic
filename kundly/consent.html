Before we begin

Kundly is a free reflection, given as a gift. Before you start writing, we need you to check four small things — the kind of things we'd want you to check if you were handing us something tender to read.

We don't need your name or your phone number to begin. Your writing stays on this device until you choose to send it. Nothing about you is stored anywhere else until you hit submit.

If you are in immediate distress or thinking about harming yourself, please do not use this tool right now. Please call iCall: 9152987821 (Mon–Sat, 8am–10pm) or AASRA: 9820466726 (24×7). Kundly will still be here tomorrow.

Only if you want to come back from another device — say, start on your phone and finish on your laptop. We'll send you one resume link and nothing else. No newsletters. No follow-up emails. Ever. If you're going to finish in one sitting on this device, you can leave this blank and we won't ask again.
