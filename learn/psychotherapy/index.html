Free for Indian Psychiatry Residents

Weave Psychotherapy Curriculum

Thirteen open-access volumes covering history, foundations, every major modality, landmark trials, and a 130-MCQ question bank. Built for residents preparing for MD, DNB, INI-CET, NEET SS and RGUHS exit exams.

Why this curriculum exists

Psychotherapy is the part of residency that's hardest to study from textbooks alone. Indian residents routinely get viva and OSCE questions on Schema Therapy, DBT skills, CBT formulation and EFT, but those modalities sit outside the standard curriculum and outside Kaplan & Sadock's depth.

These thirteen volumes pull the whole field into one place, free to read. Each one is written for the postgraduate exam, by a resident sitting them.
